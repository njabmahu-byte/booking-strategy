EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This comprehensive social media strategy positions Booking.com to capitalize on its market leadership while addressing the critical "Trust Paradox" identified in our brand analysis.

THE TRUST PARADOX

Routine bookings perform well, but service recovery reveals gaps. Strong pre-booking, weak post-booking.

4.6★ vs 2.1★

KEY OPPORTUNITY

61% of travelers book after Instagram discovery, 32% after TikTok. Platform evolution demands adaptation.

AI PERSONALIZATION

80% open to AI-designed itineraries; 94% in India. The "Era of You" demands hyper-personalized content.
